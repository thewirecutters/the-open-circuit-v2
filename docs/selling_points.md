### 5. Why This Is the “Loudest” Selling Signal to Industrial + Private‑Equity Finance

1. **Decisive Advantage Over Probabilistic Fallbacks:**  
   - In critical operations, every millisecond counts; a 0.5 % chance that a system “misses” the safe response translates into mission failure or loss of assets. The Open Circuit eliminates that probability entirely.

2. **Regulatory Alignment:**  
   - The deterministic behavior satisfies "Mission Assurance" criteria and aligns with high-reliability safety requirements for industrial systems—making adoption a compliance win rather than a legal risk.

3. **Competitive Moat for Private Equity / VC:**  
   - By proving that *zero‑latency*, hardware‑rooted determinism can be achieved without exposing trade secrets (all RTL and verification steps are openly auditable), you give investors confidence that any copied solution would still lack the underlying *timing guarantee*—thus preserving market leadership for the original provider.

4. **Cross‑Domain Scalability:**  
   - The same hardware module fits an industrial robot arm, a UAV’s flight controller, or a trading server node—allowing investors to see a unified technology stack with multiple high‑value use cases in one portfolio play.

5. **Low Integration Overhead:**  
   - Because the core logic can be synthesized into existing ASIC designs (28 nm and below) or into Xilinx/Intel FPGAs, deployment timelines shrink from years of bespoke development to weeks of a board‑level integration project.
